Founder

Ali Tanveer

After ten years of wrangling with data, information systems, effective display of quantitative information, and operational teams, Ali launched Anvil Data LLC to help others on their own data journey.

Ali led the training and operations of a medical unit in combat while serving with the US Army and transitioned those lessons into the corporate environment while leading production teams in the third party compliance investigations space. But no matter what his role was at any point in time, he consistently relied on data and tracking to measure performance, track workloads, and assess readiness.

We bring all of those lessons and more to guide you through implementing a data culture that values the right metrics and the most effective and visually pleasing way of implementing reporting and dashboards.
